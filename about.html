Who I Am

I'm Vladislav Kondratyev, a Computer Science student at UT Dallas and a builder at heart. I enjoy taking ideas from "rough sketch" to working product—whether that's a web app, an AI-powered tool, a game, or a system that makes someone's life easier.

I grew up with a strong focus on hard work and long-term goals, and that mindset still defines how I approach school, internships, and personal projects. I'm not the loudest person in the room, but I'm consistent, reliable, and serious about the things I commit to.

My Projects

I've been involved in several technical and entrepreneurial projects, including:

Real-World Experience

  • Software engineering internships in cybersecurity and education technology
  • Full-stack development for financial tools and web applications
  • Contributing to production systems used by real users

Startup & Product Ideas

  • EchoMind An AI desktop companion that learns how you work and responds with context and emotional awareness.
  • MeteorMate An AI-powered roommate matching system designed to reduce housing stress for students.
  • SongDNA / Music Tools Tools that help users analyze and discover music based on deeper metrics, not just playlists.

I'm building these products under a broader ecosystem I call MindCore—a collection of tools designed to understand and enhance how people think, work, and connect.

Hackathons & Competitions

I regularly participate in university hackathons and tech events, where I get to prototype ideas fast, work with new people, and present under pressure. These experiences constantly push me to improve my communication—explaining complex systems in simple language, writing clear documentation, and presenting ideas to people who may not be technical.

  • I care a lot about professional growth, not just "finishing assignments."
